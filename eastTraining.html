Eastern Station Sandwich Facts

Chickpea Fritter Sandwich

“Chickpea fritter is our version of falafel. There’s a layer of hummus, cucumber tomato salad, falafel balls, lots of pickled veggies, and tahini on top.” Origin: Ayr’s trip to Paris

Egg and Eggplant Sandwich

“A layer of hummus, cucumber tomato salad, fried eggplant (not battered, just eggplant fried), hard-boiled egg, tahini on top.” Origin: Taim in NYC
